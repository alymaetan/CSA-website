Sponsors

Georgia Tech's non-profit Chinese Student Association are always actively seeking sponsorship partners. We are a very dynamic and flexible group that will work to accomadate your needs. We are constantly organizing events, creating new T-Shirts, and participating in not only campus-held events, but also state and nation wide programs and activities to bring about the best Chinese culture experience as possible.

While Chinese Student Association is devoted to Chinese culture and community within Georgia Tech Institute of Technology, it welcomes and encourages people of all ages, races, and backgrounds to participate in, contribute to, and become part of CSA.

Currently, we are actively looking for sponsors. That being said, we invite you to become a sponsor. In doing so, not only will you have your sponsor status for life, but also the potential to reach out to thousands of students.

Shoot us an email at csa.gatech@gmail.com if you would like to become a sponsor!
